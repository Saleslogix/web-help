Participants Tab

Use the Participants tab to manage additional contacts and leads who are associated with an activity.

By default, when a contact or lead is added on the General tab, the contact or lead is automatically added to the Participants tab as the primary. If you select a different primary on the Participants tab, then the General tab is updated with the new primary contact or lead. Both contacts or leads remain on the Participants tab unless they are removed using the Delete button.

If you add a contact who is associated with a Saleslogix user to the Participants tab, then the associated user is automatically added to the Availability tab, so you can see if the user is free or busy. If a Saleslogix user associated with a contact is added to the Availability tab, then the contact is added to the Participants tab.

If a Saleslogix user associated with a contact is added to the Availability tab, then the contact is added to the Participants tab. If you add a contact who is associated with a Saleslogix user to the Participants tab, then the associated user is automatically added to the Availability tab, so you can see if the user is free or busy.

When you add a contact or lead to the Participants tab the activity will appear on the Activities tab on the associated contact and account detail views or lead detail view.

How Do I?

Open the Participants tab
  • Schedule or edit a meeting or phone call and click the Participants tab.
Add one or more contacts
  1. On the Participants tab, click the Add Contact button.
  2. Use the lookup to find contacts you want to add to the activity.
    Use the Lookup by and Search button to narrow the list.
  3. Select one or more contacts, and then click Add Selected.
  4. When finished, click Close.
Add one or more leads
  1. On the Participants tab, click the Add Lead button.
  2. Use the lookup to find leads you want to add to the activity.
    Use the Lookup by and Search button to narrow the list.
  3. Select one or more leads, and then click Add Selected.
  4. When finished, click Close.
Add contacts and leads
  1. On the Participants tab, click the Add Participant button.
  2. Use the Lookup Participants dialog box to find contacts or leads you want to add to the activity.
  3. Type the first few letters of the person's first name or last name and click Search.
  4. Select one or more contacts or leads, and then click Add Selected.
  5. Repeat steps 4 and 5 until you have selected all of the contacts or leads you want to add to the activity.
  6. When finished, click Close.
Change the primary contact or lead
  • In the grid, select the Primary check box next to the contact or lead you want to be the primary.
    The General tab is updated with the new primary contact or lead.
Assign a role to a participant
  1. In the grid, click in the Role column for the participant you want to edit.
  2. Type a role or click the drop-down arrow and select from the list.
Indicate the participant is an attendee

Contacts and leads added from the General tab are added to the Participants tab with the IsAttendee check box cleared to indicate that they are only associated with the activity. Contacts and leads added from the Participantstab are added with the IsAttendee check box selected to indicate that they are attendees of the meeting. You can edit the IsAttendee column for each contact and lead.

  • Select the IsAttendee check box to indicate that the contact or lead is not only associated with the activity, but is also an attendee
  • If the contact or lead is associated with the activity, but will not attend the activity, then clear the IsAttendee check box..
Remove a contact or lead
  1. Select one or more records in the list and click the Delete button.
  2. Click OK.
