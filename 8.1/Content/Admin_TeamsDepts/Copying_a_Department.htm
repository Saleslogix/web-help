Copying a Department

When you copy an existing department, all members are included in the new department.

To copy

  1. Open the Department Detail view for the department you want to copy.
  2. In the Common Tasks pane, click Copy Department.
  3. Click OK to confirm.
  4. In the Name box, type a new name for the department.
  5. If necessary, modify the team members and the teams of which the department is a member.
  6. Click Save.
