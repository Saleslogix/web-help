Deleting Campaign Stages

Deleting a stage also deletes any tasks for that stage.

To delete

  1. From the Campaign Detail view, select the Stages/Tasks tab, select the stage you want to delete, and then click Delete Stage.
  1. Click OK to confirm deletion.
