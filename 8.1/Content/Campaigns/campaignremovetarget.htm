Removing a Contact or Lead from a Campaign

If a contact or lead does not want to be included in future campaign stages, you can remove the contact or lead from the campaign. The contact or lead is still listed as a campaign target, but the status is changed to Removed.

To remove

  1. Open the Marketing tab view.
  • From the Contact, Account or Lead Detail view, click the Marketing tab. If the tab is not visible, click the More Tabs tab.
  1. In the grid, select the contact or lead that you want to remove from further stages of the campaign.
  2. Click Remove.
  3. Click OK.
