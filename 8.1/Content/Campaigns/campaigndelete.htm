Deleting a Campaign

When you delete a campaign, you also delete all of the information associated with that campaign, including response and opportunity information.

To delete a campaign

  1. Find the campaign you want to delete.
  2. On the Campaign Detail view, click Delete.

You cannot delete an active campaign. If you try to delete an active campaign, a message box will display. You must change the campaign status from "Active" before you can delete it.

  1. Click OK to permanently delete the record.
