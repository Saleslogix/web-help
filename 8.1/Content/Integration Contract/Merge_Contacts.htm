Merge Contacts

The Merge Contacts screen displays contacts that you are either merging in Saleslogix or linking an account you are to the customer you selected in an accounting system. Use this screen to create links and merge your contacts within Saleslogix or between Saleslogix and integrated accounting system contact records.

To select

  1. Open the Linking wizard or Merge Records wizard and proceed through the previous screens.
  1. Complete the Merge Contacts screen.
    BoxDescription/Action
    Unlinked Contacts from

     

    Displays contacts that are available to link from an account or account integration.

    Click Select to select contact details.

    Unlinked Contacts from

    Displays contacts that are available to link from another account or account integration.

    Click Select to select contact details.

    Link button

    Select an unlinked contact from each list, and then click Link to create a connection.

    Linked Contacts

    Displays the list of matches. Some contacts may be listed automatically based on your match criteria.

    • Click Unlink to clear the proposed link and create a new link.
    • Click Select to display the contact data fields in the source and target systems.
  2. Click Next.
