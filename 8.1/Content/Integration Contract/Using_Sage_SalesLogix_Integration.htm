Using Accounting Integration

This feature is only available if your administrator has configured Accounting Integration.

Typically, a Saleslogix user manages the integration of accounts and sales orders from Saleslogix and your accounting system by:

  • Creating an account in Saleslogix.
  • Linking the Saleslogix account to a trading company in the accounting system.
  • Creating an opportunity for the account.
  • Viewing associated sales orders
