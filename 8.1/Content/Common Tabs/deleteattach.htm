Deleting an Attachment

You can delete an attachment from a record.

If you delete an attachment from a single occurrence of a recurring activity, the attachment is deleted from the entire series.

To delete

  1. Open the Attachments tab.
  2. Select the record you want to remove and click the Delete button.

  3. Click OK.
