More Tabs

Use More Tabs to open additional tab views. More Tabs may display on any of the Detail views. You can drag and drop tabs to and from the More Tabs section.

To view a specific tab

  1. From any detail view, click More Tabs.
  2. In the list, click the tab you want to view.
