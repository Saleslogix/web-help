Performing Process Tasks

If you have configured process orchestration you can perform Process Tasks from the Common Tasks area in the Task Pane in some list views.

Process Tasks are only available if your administrator has configured process orchestration.

To perform process tasks

  1. Open a list view.
  2. In the Task Pane, Common Tasks area, if Process Tasks is available, do one of the following:
    • Click Click to Complete next to the item to open and complete.
    • Click Cancel to cancel a step.
