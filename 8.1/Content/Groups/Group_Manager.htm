Group Manager

Use the Groups list to view and manage all of your groups for a list or detail view. You can search, show hidden groups and hide groups. You can also add a new group.

How Do I?

Open Group Manager

  • From a detail or list view, click the Groups button.
Search for a group
  • In the Find box, type the first few letters of part of the group name of you are looking for, and then click Find.
    To include hidden groups in your search, select Show Hidden.
  • Click Clear to delete the contents of the Find box and start a new search.
Show or hide hidden groups in the list
  • Select the Show Hidden option to show hidden groups in the list, or clear the option to hide them.
Expand or sort a column
  • To expand a column, click and hold the column divider and drag to the size you need.
  • To sort by the Group column, click the column header. You can sort in Ascending or Descending order.
Show or hide a group
  1. Select the Show Hidden option.
  2. In the Visible column select the check boxes of the groups you want to show and clear the groups you want to hide.
