Deleting Groups

You can delete a group that you are no longer using. You can only delete groups you added. However, the system administrator can delete all groups.

 

To delete a group

  1. On a List view, right-click a group tab.
  2. Click Delete.
    If you do not have permission to delete the group this option will not be available.
  3. Click OK.
