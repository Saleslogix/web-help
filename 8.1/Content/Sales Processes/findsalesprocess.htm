Finding Sales Processes

Sales processes help you to manage your sales pipeline. You can attach a sales process to an open opportunity by selecting from a list of available processes.

To find Sales Processes

  1. Open the Opportunity Detail view.
  2. Click the Sales Process tab.
  3. Click the Sales Process drop-down arrow to see a list of available sales processes.
