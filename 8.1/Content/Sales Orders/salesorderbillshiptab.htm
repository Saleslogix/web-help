Sales Order Billing and Shipping Tab

The Bill/Ship Address tab appears on the Sales Order Detail View. Use this tab to manage the billing and shipping addresses for a sales order.

To add or edit

  1. On the Sales Order Detail View, click the Bill/Ship Address tab. If the tab is not visible, click the More Tabs tab.
  2. If necessary, edit the Billing Address information.
  1. To select a Contact, click the Find button.
  2. Click the Addressdrop-down arrow and select from the list.
  3. To edit the Address, click the Edit button. To view a map of the address, click the MapQuest button.

The associated contact's address will not be updated with these changes. To update the contact's address information, you must edit the address in the associated Contact Detail view.
  1. Add or edit the Shipping Address information.
  1. To select a Contact, click the Find button.
  2. Click the Addressdrop-down arrow and select from the list.
  3. To edit the Address, click the Edit button. To view a map of the address, click the MapQuest button.

The associated contact's address will not be updated with these changes. To update the contact's address information, you must edit the address in the associated Contact Detail view.
  1. Click Save.
