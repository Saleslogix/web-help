Editing Opportunity Product Information

Use this dialog box to enter product information such as quantity, price, or discount.

To edit

  1. From the Opportunity Detail view Products tab, click the Edit hyperlink for the product you want to edit or make your changes in the Products grid.
  2. In the Price Level box, click the drop-down arrow and select an item from the list.
  3. In the Discount box, enter a discount percentage. The number is automatically changed to a percent. For example, "50" will be "50%". If you enter ".5", then it will be ".5%".

The Adjusted Price is automatically calculated.

  1. In the Quantity box, enter the number of this product to be added.

The Extended Price is automatically calculated.

  1. Click OK.
