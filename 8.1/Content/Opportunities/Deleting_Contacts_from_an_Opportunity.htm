Removing a Contact from an Opportunity

You can remove contacts from an opportunity at any time. You can remove contacts from the Insert Opportunity dialog box or the Opportunity Detail view. Deleting a contact only removes the contact's association with a particular opportunity. The contact is not deleted from the database.

To remove a contact from the Insert Opportunity dialog box

  1. In the Contacts grid, click the contact you want to delete.
  2. Click the Remove Selected Contacts button, and then click OK to confirm the deletion.
  3. Click OK.

To remove a contact from the Opportunity Detail view

  1. Click the Contacts tab.
  2. Click the Remove button, and then click OK to confirm the deletion.
  3. Click OK.
