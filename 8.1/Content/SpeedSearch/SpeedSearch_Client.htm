SpeedSearch

SpeedSearch is a powerful tool that helps you find information stored in the Saleslogix database or external files. For example, you can search through existing tickets or procedures to help solve a customer's problem, or search for a specific document, like a sales presentation. The type of information you can search for depends on the indexes your system administrator has set up in the Administrator.

To perform a SpeedSearch

  • Do one of the following:

    • Enter search information in the Menu Bar.
    • Click the SpeedSearch button from the Ticket Details tab or Insert Ticket view.
    • On the Tools menu, click Advanced SpeedSearch.

If you experience problems opening or using SpeedSearch, contact your Saleslogix Administrator. SpeedSearch configuration and troubleshooting information is available in the Administrator Help.
