Introducing Saleslogix for Web

Saleslogix manages all aspects of the sales cycle over the Web. In addition to tracking contacts, accounts, leads, opportunities, tickets and activities, users have access to tools for automating processes, and creating e-mails, faxes, and letters using mail merge. With additional security permissions, you may also have access to administrative tools for user, team, department, and pick list management.

Depending on what your system administrator installed, you can access the Saleslogix Web Client, over the network, or from a Web phone.

This Help system explains h to use the Saleslogix Web Client. Your workspace may be different depending on your access rights or if your installation uses a customized Saleslogix Web Client.

Your system administrator can provide additional information.
