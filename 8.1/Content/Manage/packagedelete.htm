Deleting a Product Package

You can delete product packages from the list that is available when adding or editing opportunity or sales order products and product packages.

To delete

  1. On the Navigation Bar, click Sales, Support, or Administration, and then click Packages to open the Packages List view. If necessary, use the Lookup to locate a product package, and then click on the package name.
  2. On the Package Detail view, click the Delete button.
  3. Click OK.
