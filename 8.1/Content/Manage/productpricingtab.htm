Product Price Levels Tab

The system administrator and users with the appropriate role can access these features.

You can use the Price Levels tab on the Product Detail to view or manage product pricing information.

If the Price Levels tab does not appear in the Detail view, click More Tabs to access the list of available tabs. Select Price Levels and drag it into the tab area or into the user-defined area of the Detail view.

How Do I?

Add a Product Price

Edit a Product Price

Delete a Product Price
  1. Click Delete next to the price you want to delete.

  2. Click OK.

The Price Levels tab displays the following information:

  • Description - Displays the price description.
  • Price - Displays the price for the product. This column is based upon the default currency type as defined by your administrator.
  • Default - Displays the default price for the product.
