Resource Information Boxes

The resource information boxes contain information about the selected resource. You can edit or delete the information as needed.

 

Box Description/Action
Name The name of the resource )for example Conference Room 1 or PocketPC).
Type The category of the resource (for example, conference room or white board).
Is Location If selected, designates the resource as a location .
Description A brief description of the resource.
Coordinator The person responsible for coordinating use of the resource. Click the Find button to find the person or team who manages the resource.
Coordinator Phone The coordinator's phone number.
Coordinator Email The coordinator's e-mail address. To send an e-mail message to the coordinator, click the E-mail button..
